Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

A recipe for a good peanut butter and jelly sandwich is something that all at home chefs need to have. While cullinary experts will argue until the end of time about which jelly one should use, the key to a good sandwich is the ratio.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly or jam (any flavor)

Steps

  1. Plunge knife into jar of peanut butter. Scoop out a heaping amount of peanut butter and spread onto one side of bread.
  2. Repeat step one and spread onto one side of other slice of bread.
  3. Using the same knife, remove a knifeful of jelly from jar and spread onto the peanut butter side of only one slice of bread.
